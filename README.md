## 📋 Overview

**TradeVault** is a professional B2B wholesale platform designed to bridge the gap between suppliers and business buyers. It focuses on high-volume transactions and bulk purchasing with a visual identity centered on trust and reliability.

### 🎯 Core Purpose

TradeVault serves as a centralized hub for bulk purchasing, enabling businesses to source products directly from verified suppliers. The platform handles complex B2B trade requirements such as tiered pricing and minimum order quantities while maintaining a streamlined, user-friendly interface.

## ✨ Features

### 🏠 Frontend Marketplace
- **Hero Banner** - Showcases curated "Supplier Finds" to highlight top-tier partners
- **Advanced Product Grid** - Specialized filters for categories and MOQ
- **Bulk Pricing Logic** - Transparent tiered pricing on product pages
- **Supplier Storefronts** - Dedicated pages for individual suppliers
- **Buyer Support Hub** - Footer-integrated resources for procurement

### 👑 Admin Dashboard
- Complete user management (buyers, suppliers, admins)
- Product management (add, edit, delete)
- Order tracking and management
- Platform analytics and charts
- User role management

### 🏭 Supplier Dashboard
- Product catalog management
- Order management with buyer details
- Sales analytics and charts
- Commission tracking
- Performance insights
- AI-powered sales recommendations

### 🛒 Buyer Features
- Product browsing with filtering
- Shopping cart for bulk orders
- Secure checkout with Stripe integration
- Order history and tracking
- Tiered pricing visualization

### 📊 Analytics & Charts
- Revenue trend analysis (Line Chart)
- Sales by category (Pie Chart)
- Top selling products (Bar Chart)
- Order status distribution (Doughnut Chart)
- Weekly order volume (Bar Chart)
- Average order value trends (Line Chart)
- User growth metrics (Bar Chart)
- Supplier performance (Bar Chart)
